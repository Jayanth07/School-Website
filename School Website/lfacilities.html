Hostel

1. Counselling Sessions & Motivation Sessions
	-Every day there are 4 extra study hours (1 hour in the morning & 3 hours in the evening)
	-Thus there are a minimum of 100 study hours per month per class
	-These study hours are under the supervision of concerned subject teachers & HODs
	-Individual attention is paid to slow learners and gifted children during study hours
	-Motivation sessions for handwriting improvement are conducted by the experts like Mr.Mallikarjuna Rao
	-Memory improvement techniques are conducted by Guiness World record holder Mr.Jaya Simha
2. Accommodation
	-Dormitory type of accommodation with 6 bunker beds can accommodate 12 students per room
	-A/C & Non-A/C facility
	-Solar water heaters for hot water
	-Well ventillated rooms
	-Chief warden's act as locus parents by looking into all the needs of boarders
3. Food & Water
	-Hygienic dining hall
	-Food menu is approved by NIN (National Institute of Nutrition, Hyderabad)
	-We have our own mineral water plant
	-Own Dairy Form at Gosala Campus to cater the needs of all the boarders
	-Menu includes break fast, lunch, snacks and dinner ( both Veg & Non -Veg)
	-Milk and seasonol fruits are given daily to students
4. Play Ground
	-Both indoor & outdoor games like T.T, Carroms, Chess, Badminton, Volleyball, Basketball, Throwball, Cricket, Tenni Coit, Kho-Kho, Kabaddi etc are available.
5. Extra Curricular Activities
	-Yoga classes & physical excercises everyday.
	-Karate, music & dance classes for boarders thrice in a week.
6. Recreation
	-Weekend movies in open air theatre / auditorium
	-Fun games, spot dance programmes & quiz programmes at weekends
	-Educational tour / Field trips for the students, once in a month
7. Dispensary
	-Well experienced Doctors attend the schools(OP) twice in a day, except on Sundays
	-Well trained Nurses (male & female), Pharmacist & Compounder are available
	-One lakh worth of antibiotics and other medicines are available in dispensary
	-Tie-ups with local super speciality hospitals in case of emergencies

Bus Transportation

Sri Chaitanya Techno Schools believes in safety and comfort of their students.

The fleet of buses provides a comfortable seating for 40 students. The buses have fulfilled the requisite of Government regulation standards. A well equipped first aid box is kept handy on the buses. The bus attenders take care of the seating arrangement for the children. Trained drivers drive the buses on their respective routes.

Sri Chaitanya's fleet of buses cover the entire length and breadth of the twin cities.
